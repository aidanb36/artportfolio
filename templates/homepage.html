Customise it

If you have a specific request or concept that you would like to bring to life, I encourage you to provide as many details as possible in your request. The more information you can provide about your vision, including stylistic choices, preferred medium, and desired size, the better I will be able to understand and bring your ideas to life. I am dedicated to working with you to create something truly special and one-of-a-kind, and I am eager to hear your unique artistic ideas. So don't hesitate to reach out to me with your request.

Receive it

In order to ensure a smooth and efficient process for obtaining your newly created art piece, here are a few steps to follow: First, confirm the delivery method with me. If the piece is being mailed to you, consider purchasing insurance or tracking for added protection during transit. Once you have received the piece, take the time to carefully inspect it for any damage that may have occurred during handling or transit. If you notice any issues, bring them to my attention as soon as possible so that we can address them. I hope that these steps will help you to smoothly obtain your newly created art piece and that you enjoy it!

About me

I'm a recent graduate of Eastern Connecticut State University, where I studied anthropology. I have a passion for art and have been creating it throughout my life. My main areas of expertise are painting, sculpture, and figure drawing, but I am also expanding my skills in digital art and animation. I am known for my handmade cards which I can customize to fit any theme or occasion.
