About me

I'm a recent graduate of Eastern Connecticut State University, where I studied anthropology. I have a passion for art and have been creating it throughout my life. My main areas of expertise are painting, sculpture, and figure drawing, but I am also expanding my skills in digital art and animation. I am known for my handmade cards which I can customize to fit any theme or occasion.


Request a quote

Are you looking to bring a new art piece to life? I would love to collaborate with you on your vision! If you have an idea for an art project that you would like to see come to fruition, don't hesitate to send me a request. We can work together to bring your creative ideas to life and create something truly unique and special. Whether you have a specific theme or medium in mind, or you just have a general idea that you would like to explore further, I am open to discussing all possibilities. Let's make something beautiful together!
